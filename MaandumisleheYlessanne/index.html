Despite its athletic skills, the RS6 Avant is still a fully functional wagon with room to seat five in comfort, luxury class-appropriate interior materials and features, and contemporary infotainment and technology. The rear hatch opens to 30 cubic feet of cargo space, fulfilling the wagon promise. Its brawny proportions are accented with a number of aggressive aerodynamic bits, but it still manages to stop short of appearing ostentatious.
